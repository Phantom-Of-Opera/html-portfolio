The top 3 Movies of Mr French Toast !

Three movies that I really liked

(and so much more that I loved...)

  • Itineraire d'un enfant gate

    A great movie about the importance of not losing itself in life and always be aware that we can change anything if we really want it

  • Aliens

    A very classic set of movies that walk the fine line of keeping you on the edge of your seat while asking a few good questions about ourselves

  • Love Actually

    A brilliant british comedy full of humor and feelings. A classic but a very good moment to spend with these characters stories
